  • owner: – EETC
  • Executive Main Contractor: – XD||EGEMAC.
  • Space: – 250,000-Meter Square.

El-Eqtsadia (2) Substation 500/220/66/22 kV
  • Location: Suez Governorate
  • Owner: Egyptian Electricity Transmission Company (EETC)
  • Executive Main Contractor: XD||EGEMAC

Summary:

  • El-Eqtsadiya (2) S/S is considered as one of the largest substations which executed by XD||EGEMAC, with a rating of 500/220/66/22 kV, the project is located in the Industrial Zone-Suez Governorate; the main purpose and objective from El-Eqtsadiya (2) substation is a reinforcement of the 500 kV electrical network to face the increase in the electrical energy demands and secure reliable power supply with minimum losses of transmitted power in the national grid.
  • The substation consists of:
    • (8) Eight 500kV double circuits; (4) Four of them are used to connect the substation with both Sokhna PP 500kV & Administrative Capital 500kV.
    • (8) Eight 220kV double circuits; (4) Four of them are used to connect the substation with both Egypt Cement & Ataka stations.
    • The substation is located on an area of 160,000 square meters, northwest of the Gulf of Suez.
    • The substation mainly consists of the following equipment:
    • (10) bays 500 kV GIS.
    • (14) bays 200 kV GIS.
    • (16) bays 66 kV GIS.
    • (7) 250 MVA, 500/220/22 kV Single phase Autotransformers.
    • (3) l75 MVA, 220/66 kV Power Transformers.
    • (4) 40 MVA, 66/22 kV Power Transformers.

XD||EGEMAC’s Mobile S/S Projects

Mobile substations are commonly used when it`s necessary to supply electrical power to a particular project as temporary solution till having the main substation is constructed or upgraded, During Mega projects execution period, emergency weather circumstances or for emergency needs to supply occasional loads for events or additional consumption in a specific area or supply remote places can’t be fed from the power grid. Mobile substations can be assembled, transported, and operated in a short time with a minimum space available without civil work and with lower cost and operating effort in comparison to the regular substations. A mobile station is a fully self-contained trailer station consisting of H.V & M.V switchgear, transformers, and outdoor equipment, along with metering fixtures, protective devices, auxiliary feeding equipment, and lightning protection. XD||EGEMAC had successfully supplied fully operational & functional 5×60 MVA mobile substations in Egypt for the Egyptian Electricity Transmission Company (EETC) under a contract signed in April 2019, within a 6-month completion period which had been completely fulfilled

The five mobile substations are currently operating successfully in the Egyptian power grid and are distributed as follows:

  • One 220/22/22 kV substation in Marsa Alam.
  • Two 220/22/22 kV substations in Barnes.
  • Two 220/11/11 kV substations in Toshka.

XD||EGEMAC believes it can provide a full technical solution for the implementation of other mobile substations with different loads and capacities fully customized to meet the client’s requirements and compatible with IEC standards.

XD||EGEMAC expresses its appreciation to MOE & IT`S Affiliated company EETC for their trust & support in offering such an opportunity for participating in such an important project.
